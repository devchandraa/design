01

Overview

-ART FOR ALL-

Art&Kart is a mobile app design which provides art-lovers and artists a common platform to buy and sell paintings. The idea is to bring artists and art galleries from around the world to do their business with interested buyers and make it engaging at the same time. From traditional to digital artists, everyone can post their ideas and also follow their inspirations.

Initial Understanding

From initial research, 3 primary conclusions have been drawn:

1. The world of design including graphics, gaming, fashion interior as well as modern infrastructure is growing at a tremendous pace and so the number of people intrigued in it. On the other hand, technological aid is still lagging to spread the arts from various genre and culture to around the globe.


2. It is been a problem for art-lovers to explore arts, even locally, due to various reasons such as busy urban lifestyle, no common platform to know about any art related event nearby, long traveling time and others.


3. Lastly, there are several platforms that engage people to experience art virtually, but there's no option to buy any at the same time and vice-versa.

Challenges Coverage

How to distinguish the e-commerce part of the app from networking part?

To have a clear picture of the app's information architecture in the user mind, it was important to differentiate the e-commerce from the networking segment. But at the same time, it has to be kept in mind that the artists should have an option to sell their artwork using networking area.

How much information would be enough to keep the balance between just-to-know (buyers) and want-to-know (professionals) users?

In apps like 'Unravel Van Gogh', the primary focus is to present artwork in all its glory. But here the requirements are conflicting for the two ends of users, customers and professionals seeking inspiration. This is resolved by separating the information according to the flow, i.e., users on purchasing page would have product related information and users on artstation would have art-related information.

What should be the major categories over which arts has to divided?

There are multiple categories which I found on Google Art & Culture site, over which arts (paintings) can be segmented such as on theme, medium, time, color, artists, etc. But which segment would be best for marketing purposes?

04

Prototype

-HOME PAGE: FOR BUYERS-

Since e-commerce is the primary goal of the app, complete home-page is designed to explore different categories of paintings on the basis of theme, collections, medium and others. Digital art is also an emerging market and special marketing strategies would be applied to that. However, some of the users might be interested in original paintings, and there’s option for that also. Users can navigate through the page easily using the shortcut button at the top of the page. Sharing option is also provided to the users.

-ORDERING PROCESS-

Ordering paintings is the core process for the app and therefore usability testing has been performed for this part before prototyping. ‘Add to cart’ overlay and page (both, for non-interrupted flow), multiple payment option, address changing option and review & confirmation page after purchasing have been included after usability testing.

-ARTSTATION: FOR ARTISTS-

Artstation is designed to inspire artists, people in design fields and even non-artist users. To engage users and communities, this section is designed to post, drop stories, follow artists and communities, organize live sessions and also get notified for any live events in which user might be interested. Users can even subscribe their artworks to sell through this networking page.


-USER'S PROFILE PAGE-

Only 3 buttons on the bottom-navbar are provided on the home-page: Home, artstation and profile. So, the information or settings not covered under home and artstation must be provided on the profile page. Other than user profile description, users can also see their posts and arts on shop. Option provided in this page include all major settings for this app especially for e-commerce business.

05

Reflection

-ART FOR ALL-

Usability metrics like learnability, effectiveness and insightfulness have been tested on users and received positive reviews. Since it was my first project, there was a lot to learn starting from understanding users’ problems, ideating solutions, iterating over design decisions to documenting solution.


1. Goal driven designs: Whole project is based on creating interaction among artists and buyers, and so is the design. Improvements in this app will be made as I receive more feedbacks.


2. Culture and modernity: • Culture and modernity: Originally the idea was to encourage the local (and traditional) artists to do business with interested buyers worldwide. The purpose of artstation is to share artworks and ideas to large group of people.


3. Art inspiration: My encounter with targeted users during initial research suggested that students from art background are seeking inspiration, and want to follow the artists’ work as well as attend their events. This app is designed to fulfill all these needs.
